**Purpose**
A Progressive Web App (PWA) designed for a hockey association to manage its tournaments effectively. The primary users are parents, players, coaches, and supporters, who access the app to obtain tournament information like fixtures, match results, and pool and stage results.

**Details**
The home screen must must be a landing page with the welcome message and an announcement section under the welcome message. The announcements will be for messages from the association to communicate any information, like fixture changes.

There must be a franchise page where all franchises are listed. Franchises have a short description,information on the manager, logo, contact details, location, and a get in touch button that launches an email enquiry.

The home screen must also show tournaments: past, current, coming up

The tournament page must have a link to the rules, show the participating teams, fixtures, results, and awards.
When you click on a team you go to the team page which includes: the logo, name, strap line (optional), manager, divisions. Clicking in the division shows the coach and players.
The awards are handed out per division. Accolades include: the top scorers (based on goals scored), top goalkeeper (based on clean sheets)
